True North Property Services

A family-owned landscaping business built from the ground up two brothers, a shared work ethic, and a commitment to doing things right.

True North Property Services started with two brothers who wanted to build something of their own and do landscaping the right way.

After several years of hands-on experience, they began taking on jobs themselves — starting with a few neighbors, then referrals, and steadily growing from there. From the beginning, the focus was simple: show up, do clean, detailed work, and be someone customers could rely on.

That same approach still drives the business today. The company now serves homeowners across Northeast Ohio, handling everything from routine maintenance to full landscape installs. Each job is approached with the same mindset — take care of the property, pay attention to the details, and leave it better than it was found.

What started small has grown through consistency, word of mouth, and doing the work the right way — and that standard hasn't changed.
