Bed Installation

Want a new planting bed where there's currently just lawn? We handle everything from the layout and design to sod removal, edging, and ground cover — so it's ready to plant the day we finish.

  • Bed shape and layout designed with you
  • Sod removal and soil prep
  • Clean edging to define the border
  • Mulch or rock for ground cover
1

Design the Layout

We walk your yard with you and mark out the bed shape before any digging starts.

2

Remove Existing Sod

Grass and weeds are removed from the bed area and properly disposed of.

3

Prep the Soil

Soil is tilled and amended as needed. Topsoil or compost is added if required.

4

Edge, Mulch & Finish

Clean edges are cut, ground cover is applied, and the bed is ready for plants.
