Bush & Shrub Trimming

Well-shaped shrubs frame your home and keep your yard looking clean. We trim with the plant's natural form in mind — not just buzzing everything into a box — and clean up every clipping when we're done.

  • Shaping and sizing of shrubs, hedges, and bushes
  • Dead wood removal for plant health
  • Reduction of overgrown bushes
  • All clippings removed and beds left tidy
1

Assess Each Plant

We look at the species, size, and desired shape before any cuts are made.

2

Trim with Precision

We use hand shears and hedge trimmers to shape each plant properly and evenly.

3

Remove Dead Wood

Dead, crossed, or damaged branches are removed to improve plant health.

4

Clean Up

All clippings are raked, blown, and removed. Your beds are left clean and tidy.
