Mulch Installation

Fresh mulch keeps weeds down, holds moisture, and makes your beds look clean and finished. We handle everything from prepping the beds to spreading and edging so it looks great when we leave.

  • Bed cleanup and light weeding before we start
  • Choice of color: natural hardwood, black, red, or brown dyed
  • Applied at the right depth for weed control and moisture retention
  • Old mulch removal available on request
