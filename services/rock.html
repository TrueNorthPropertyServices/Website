Rock & Stone

Rock is a great low-maintenance alternative to mulch — it looks sharp and you never have to replace it. We help you pick the right type and get it installed cleanly, with landscape fabric underneath to keep weeds out.

  • River rock, pea gravel, lava rock, and crushed stone
  • Landscape fabric to prevent weed growth
  • Delivery and spreading included
  • Edging to keep everything contained
1

Choose Your Rock

We show you options and help you pick the right type, size, and color for your space.

2

Prep the Area

We weed the beds and install landscape fabric to keep weeds from growing up through the rock.

3

Deliver & Spread

Rock is delivered and evenly spread at the appropriate depth for your project.

4

Edge & Finish

Borders are installed or cleaned up to keep everything contained and looking sharp.
